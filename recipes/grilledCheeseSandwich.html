Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a timeless dish that is universally loved. It's simple, yet absolutely satisfying. The classic version involves sandwiching a slice of cheese between two pieces of bread, which is then toasted on a skillet until golden brown and the cheese melts to gooey perfection. The outside is crisp and buttery, while the inside is irresistibly cheesy. It's the ultimate comfort food that pairs perfectly with a bowl of tomato soup or a side of chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (like American, Cheddar, or Mozzarella)
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place a slice of cheese between the bread slices with the buttered side facing outwards.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place the sandwich on the skillet, and cook until the bread is toasted golden and the cheese is melted, flipping halfway.
  4. Remove from the skillet, cut in half and serve warm.
