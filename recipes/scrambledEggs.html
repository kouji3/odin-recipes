Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is a classic, quick, and easy breakfast dish that is both nourishing and versatile. This simple rendition uses a few staple ingredients: eggs, salt, pepper, and butter. Adding cheese is optional, but itâ€™s a delicious way to add an extra layer of flavor and texture. The key to perfect scrambled eggs is cooking on medium-low heat and constant stirring which results in soft curds of gentle, creamy eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup of grated Cheddar cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit without stirring for a few seconds until it starts to set around the edges.
  4. Stir the eggs continuously with a spatula, pushing them from one side of the skillet to the other until large curds begin to form and the eggs are almost cooked, but still slightly runny.
  5. Remove from heat and sprinkle with cheese, if using. The residual heat will continue to cook them slightly and melt the cheese. Serve hot.
